## Primary Buyers
### Venue Owner
Wants more control, clarity, and revenue predictability. Usually feels trapped in day-to-day admin and manual follow-up.

### Operations Team Lead
Feels the pain of scattered communication, booking confusion, and workflow inconsistency. Wants centralization and accountability.

### Event Manager / Event Operations Lead
Needs event execution to be repeatable and coordinated. Values timelines, checklists, and role clarity.

## Common Pain Profile
- leads go unanswered
- no after-hours responsiveness
- manual booking chaos
- team communication breakdowns
- inconsistent guest experience
- weak follow-up and marketing retention

## What They Want
- one system that makes the venue feel organized
- less dependence on memory and manual effort
- more predictable lead handling
- clarity across staff and events
- evidence that the system will produce real outcomes
