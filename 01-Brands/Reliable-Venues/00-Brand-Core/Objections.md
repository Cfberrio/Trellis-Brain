## Likely Objections
- "We already use other tools."
- "We still need staff, so how much does this really solve?"
- "Will this work for my kind of venue?"
- "How long will implementation take?"
- "I do not want to get locked into another vendor."
- "I am not sure my problem is big enough for this."

## Framing Answers
### Existing tools
Reliable Venues is not asking the venue to abandon everything blindly. The system can connect and organize what already exists.

### Staff
The pitch is not "replace staff." The pitch is "use staff where human judgment matters and automate repetitive workflow."

### Venue fit
The positioning says the systems scale from boutique spaces to larger event centers.

### Contract resistance
Current commercial positioning emphasizes:
- no retainers
- no long-term contracts
- performance-based framing
