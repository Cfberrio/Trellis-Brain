# OEV Sales Process

## Parent
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/01-Systems/Sales/Sales-Home|OEV Sales Home]]

## Related
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/01-Systems/Finance/Payment-Rules|OEV Payment Rules]]

## Core Sequence
1. Capture contact and event details.
2. Confirm fit: date, guest count, event type, rules.
3. Confirm availability or closest alternatives.
4. Send booking / quote path.
5. Ask for 50% deposit when qualified.
6. Confirm booking.
7. Select add-ons.
8. Hand off to pre-event ops.
9. Capture proof after event.

## Standard Principle
Do not let the lead remain in vague interest. Move them to a binary next step.
