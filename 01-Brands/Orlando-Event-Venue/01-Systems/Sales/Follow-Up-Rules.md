# OEV Follow-Up Rules

## Parent
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/01-Systems/Sales/Sales-Home|OEV Sales Home]]

## Rules
- Continue in SMS as the main decision thread.
- Speed matters most immediately after inquiry.
- Use truthful scarcity only.
- Stop generic blasting if the lead replies.
- Always orient toward the next decision step.

## Default Cadence
- Day 0: confirmation + quote + deposit CTA.
- Same day: two follow-up nudges if silent.
- Next two days: two touches/day.
- Next four days: one touch/day.
- Then long-term nurture.
