# OEV Beverage Menu

**Status:** Active  
**Policy:** Bar service is available as a paid add-on for all private events. Client selects a package at booking. Admin assigns the bar service vendor to the booking from the backend — vendor receives notification (email + SMS) via GHL, same system as cleaning staff assignment. No outside alcohol or outside bartenders permitted.  
**Sales framing:** "Bar service, handled. Choose your package and we take care of the rest."

## Quoting Logic

After fit is confirmed and guest count is locked, present bar service as an add-on.

**Example — 50 guests, Signature Bar:**
- 50 × $32.13 = **$1,606.50**
- Add to invoice as line item: `Bar Service — Signature Bar`

**Pricing display rule:** Always quote per-guest. Never quote as a lump sum (e.g., do not say "$1,606" — say "$32.13/guest").
**Discount rule:** Bar service is excluded from multi-day and corporate discounts.
**Timing rule:** Bar package must be confirmed and added to the invoice at booking — not day-of.
