# OEV Closing Logic

## Parent
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/01-Systems/Sales/Sales-Home|OEV Sales Home]]

## Core Close
If the event fits and the date is available, the next step is the 50% deposit to hold the date.

## Why This Works
The close is not framed as pressure. It is framed as the operational commitment required to reserve limited inventory.

## Valid Scarcity Reasons
- real hold expiration,
- competing inquiry on same slot,
- limited tech resource,
- objectively tight peak-date inventory.
