# OEV Objection Handling

## Parent
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/01-Systems/Sales/Sales-Home|OEV Sales Home]]

## Handling Principle
Clarify fit, clarify cost, clarify rules, and clarify next step. Do not over-negotiate against the brand's economics.

## Standard Reframe
Ask: "What is your main concern right now?"

Then resolve according to bucket:
- price,
- rules,
- timing,
- tour,
- A/V,
- approval from another decision maker.
