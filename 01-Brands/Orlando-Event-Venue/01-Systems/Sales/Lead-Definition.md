# OEV Lead Definition

## Parent
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/01-Systems/Sales/Sales-Home|OEV Sales Home]]

## Qualified Lead Conditions
A qualified OEV lead:
- has a date or date window,
- fits capacity,
- accepts major constraints,
- has economics that can reach minimum booking threshold,
- and has a real event reason where failure is costly.

## Unqualified or Low-Quality Lead Signals
- wants liquor when policy forbids it,
- exceeds capacity,
- only wants the cheapest possible room,
- resists rules strongly,
- has no date seriousness,
- refuses the minimum economics.
