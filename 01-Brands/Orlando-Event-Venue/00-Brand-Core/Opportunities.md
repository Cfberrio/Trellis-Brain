## Highest-Leverage Opportunities
### 1. Reconcile public website with internal booking rules
The public site appears to communicate a simpler booking promise than the internal deposit-first operating model. This must be reconciled to avoid trust loss and downstream confusion.

### 2. Turn production into a clearer structured upsell menu
The production ladder is strong, but it should be surfaced more cleanly in website and booking flows.

### 3. Use availability + quote as the main lead magnet, not generic inquiries
The business already knows what callers want. The funnel should reflect that.

### 4. Build recurring corporate rebooking as a formal expansion path
Repeat bookings should become a designed offer, not an improvised follow-up.

### 5. Instrument dashboard visibility around deposit conversion, attach rate, and cancellation quality
The right dashboard should make booking friction visible, not just report raw lead counts.
