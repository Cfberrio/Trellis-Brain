# OEV KPIs

## Parent
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/00-Brand-Core/Brand-Home|Orlando Event Venue - Brand Home]]

## Related
- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/05-Operations/OEV-Admin-Command-Center|OEV Admin Command Center]]

## Acquisition KPIs
- Inbound leads by source.
- Missed call rate.
- Percent of leads captured into CRM.

## Conversion KPIs
- Lead to confirmed availability.
- Confirmed to Hourly/Daily chosen.
- Chosen to deposit paid.
- Deposit paid to event held.
- Review request to review completed.

## Fulfillment KPIs
- Pending to Confirmed SLA.
- Confirmed to Pre-Event Ready SLA.
- Add-ons locked at least 48h before event.
- Inspection SLA completion.

## Economics KPIs
- Average booking value.
- Add-on attach rate.
- Bar service attach rate (% of private event bookings that include a bar package).
- Bar package distribution (which package — House / Essential / Signature / Bespoke — is selected most).
- Gross margin per booking.

## Weekly North-Star Five
1. Inbound leads captured.
2. Decision Packet delivered rate.
3. Deposit conversion rate.
4. Average booking value plus attach rate.
5. Cancellation rate plus review completion rate.
