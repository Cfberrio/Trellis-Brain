## Rules
- Say what the next step is.
- State restrictions cleanly, not emotionally.
- Use concrete numbers when useful.
- Prefer clarity over persuasion tricks.
- Do not hide mandatory fees or requirements.
- Use "beer & wine only" exactly and consistently until policy changes.
- Use "50% deposit to hold the date" consistently until reconciled otherwise.
- Say "up to 90 guests" consistently.
- When talking about production, present it as optional support or required vehicle depending on the ask.

## Words To Favor
professional, smooth, clear, flexible, supported, compliant, straightforward, optional, held, confirmed, included.

## Words To Avoid
cheap, party room, luxury destination, stress-free magic, all-inclusive fantasy language, no-rules language.
