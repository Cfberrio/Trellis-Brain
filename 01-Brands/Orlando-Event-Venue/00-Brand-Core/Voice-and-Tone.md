- [[01-Brands/Orlando-Event-Venue/00-Brand-Core/Brand-Home|Orlando Event Venue - Brand Home]]

## Voice Definition
OEV should sound:
- clear,
- composed,
- practical,
- trustworthy,
- logistics-aware,
- professionally warm without sounding fluffy.

## Tone by Situation
### Website / landing pages
Confident, simple, fast to understand, credibility-first.

### Pricing and rules
Direct, calm, transparent, non-defensive.

### Booking and follow-up
Helpful, decisive, next-step oriented.

### Objection handling
Empathetic but structured. Do not ramble or over-explain.

## What To Avoid
- Overly luxurious fantasy language.
- Generic banquet-hall language.
- Overly casual slang.
- Apologetic language when stating rules.
- Hypey direct-response language that reduces trust.
