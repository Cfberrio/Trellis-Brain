## Hard Constraints
- Maximum capacity: 90 guests.
- Hourly bookings require a 4-hour minimum.
- Beer and wine only; no liquor.
- Technical equipment usage requires the relevant production package.
- Setup and breakdown expectations must be accepted.
- The buyer must fit the minimum economics.

## Commercial Constraints
- Hourly floor starts at $759 including required cleaning.
- Daily floor starts at $1,098 including required cleaning.
- Optional add-ons increase total spend and should be framed early when relevant.

## Strategic Constraint
OEV should not chase leads that only want the cheapest empty room. The business is built around certainty, clarity, and reduced execution risk.
