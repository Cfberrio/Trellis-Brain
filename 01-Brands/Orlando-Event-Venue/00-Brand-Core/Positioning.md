## Positioning Statement
Orlando Event Venue is a professional, flexible, sub-90-capacity Orlando venue designed for corporate gatherings, workshops, presentations, and controlled private events that need a clean booking path, clear rules, and optional production support.

## Positioning Priority
Primary lane:
- corporate meetings,
- trainings,
- workshops,
- presentations,
- team gatherings.

Secondary lane:
- compliant private celebrations,
- baby showers,
- birthdays,
- anniversaries,
- community events that fit the rules and economics.

## What OEV Is Not
- not a chaos-friendly party venue,
- not a liquor-first nightlife space,
- not a bargain-basement room rental,
- not a full-service event planner,
- not a wedding-heavy identity brand.

## Positioning Logic
OEV wins when the buyer has a fixed date, real reputational or logistical risk, and enough budget to value certainty. The positioning should therefore emphasize:
- confidence,
- professionalism,
- speed,
- clarity,
- reduced event risk,
- optional production capability.

## Positioning Guardrail
If future content starts to make OEV look like a general celebration venue first, the positioning has drifted.
