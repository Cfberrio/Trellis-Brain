## One-line models
**For parents:** Discipline Rift helps parents of kids 6–12 get their child active, confident, and connected through fun-first, on-campus after-school sports seasons led by trained coaches and designed for beginner-friendly development.

**For schools:** Discipline Rift helps schools increase youth sports participation with low-lift, on-campus after-school seasons that are parent-funded and operationally clean.

**For the business:** Revenue comes from parent-paid, per-player, fixed-term seasons delivered on campus with the school providing access, space, and distribution support.

## Canon decisions currently locked
- Canon price per season: **$129**.
- Volleyball is the current spearhead sport because it has the strongest demand, most teams, and most students.
- Tennis, pickleball, and flag football also fill, but volleyball remains the default proof-of-demand sport.
- Communication, GHL, and tools are intentionally excluded from this version.

## What Discipline Rift is really selling
Discipline Rift is not selling “sports practice” in the abstract. It is selling the easiest credible yes for youth sports participation: on-campus, after-school, beginner-friendly sports seasons that reduce parent logistics, lower intimidation, and create visible progress without the intensity and friction of club sports.

## Strategic center of gravity
The model wins when four things stay true at the same time:
1. The school experiences low operational burden.
2. The parent feels the offer is simple, safe, and worth the fixed seasonal commitment.
3. The child feels the program is beginner-safe and fun enough to return.
4. Delivery produces enough progress, belonging, and consistency that re-enrollment becomes natural.

## Operational implications
- The model is logistics-driven as much as it is sports-driven.
- The dismissal/pickup flow is part of the offer, not just an operational detail.
- Coach consistency and practice quality are part of the sales engine because they increase trust and retention.
- Attendance is not just a delivery KPI; it is a leading indicator of retention and future season conversion.
