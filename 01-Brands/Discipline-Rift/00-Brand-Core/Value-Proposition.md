Discipline Rift creates value by solving the biggest blocker to youth sports participation: **friction**. It removes parent logistics, reduces beginner fear, and packages sports into a predictable, low-pressure seasonal commitment that fits normal family life.

## The three strongest sources of value
1. **On-campus convenience** — the child is already at school, so the parent avoids the off-campus sports burden.
2. **Beginner-safe environment** — kids can start without feeling behind or embarrassed.
3. **Coach-led visible progress** — the season has structure, skill progression, and repeatable sessions that create proof of improvement.

## Why parents say yes
Parents do not need to say yes to “sports” in general. They need to say yes to a sports option that feels manageable, safe, and worth the fixed price.

## Why schools say yes
Schools say yes when participation goes up without creating supervision headaches, staff burden, or parent confusion.
