## Core weekly numbers
1. Leads created.
2. Engaged leads and engagement rate.
3. Paid enrollments.
4. Lead-to-enroll conversion.
5. Engaged-to-enroll conversion.
6. Enrollments per school.
7. Average attendance / first-session show rate.
8. Re-enroll intent by end of season.

## Metrics that matter most operationally
- First-session attendance rate.
- Attendance drop-off by week.
- Intervention touches sent after attendance drops.
- Enrollment by school and by sport.
- Cost / friction objections frequency.
