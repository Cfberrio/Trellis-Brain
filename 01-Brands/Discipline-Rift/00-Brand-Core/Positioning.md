## Core positioning
Discipline Rift is an on-campus after-school sports brand for kids ages 6–12. It is positioned against off-campus friction, club intensity, and unstructured after-school time.

## What DR is not
- Not a travel/club program.
- Not an elite-first sports academy.
- Not generic aftercare.
- Not a loose enrichment class with weak skill progression.
- Not a parent logistics burden.

## Competitive contrast
DR wins when parents compare it to club, parks, YMCA, or “do nothing” alternatives and conclude that DR is the cleanest path to safe participation. It also wins when schools compare it to vendors that create front-office confusion, pickup chaos, or supervision risk and conclude that DR is operationally cleaner.

## Positioning statement
Discipline Rift makes youth sports easier to start and easier to stick with by removing the biggest barrier—logistics—while replacing pressure with fun-first, coach-led, beginner-safe progression.

## School-facing positioning
For schools, DR is a low-lift sports participation partner. The offer is parent-funded, coach-led, space-efficient, and built to avoid staffing headaches.

## Parent-facing positioning
For parents, DR is the easiest credible sports yes after school: no driving, no tournament lifestyle, no elite pressure, and no confusing commitment structure.
