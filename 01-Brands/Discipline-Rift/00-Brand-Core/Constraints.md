## Fixed constraints from current model
- Ages: 6–12.
- Parent-paid seasonal model.
- School must provide access, permission, and usable space.
- The model works best when after-school pickup / aftercare flow already exists.
- The offer is not designed for elite-travel expectations.
- Delivery must stay beginner-friendly and low-pressure.
- The school experience must stay low-lift and operationally clean.

## Business constraints
- One sport may headline commercially, but operations must remain capable across multiple sports.
- Delivery quality and coach consistency cannot drift without damaging sales and retention.
- Attendance is a fragile leading indicator; poor first-session show rate weakens retention.
