### Page 4

Block 4 (12 min) — Fluency Reps: Catching Low /
Medium / High Ladder
Goal: lots of successful catches at different levels
Setup
Partners on spots 5 yards apart
1 ball per pair
How to Run (3 rounds x 4 minutes)
LOW round (below waist) — use rake/scoop or just "hands ready" depending
on your cue
MEDIUM round (chest) — diamond hands if thatʼs your cue
HIGH round (above chest/face) — diamond hands + soft throws
Ladder rule (game feel)
If a pair gets 3 catches in a row, both take one step back
If they drop 2 in a row, take one step in
Coach focus: only correct the cue of the week + "soft throw."
Micro-CFU 10 sec
"When do you use diamond hands?" (medium/high)
"When do you use rake hands?" (low)
Block 5 (13 min) — Decision + Communication Reps: 
Name + Ball Challenge
Same partners, now add pressure (still lots of reps).
How to Play
Thrower calls: "Name… READY!"
Receiver answers: "BALL!" and shows target hands

### Page 5

Thrower chooses the level (low/medium/high) and throws softly
Scoring (per pair)
1 point = catch with correct hands
1 point = perfect talk (name + ball)
First to 10 points wins, then switch partners
"Out" rule (drops)
Drop = JOB ZONE 10 seconds
Stay-on Job 30 sec max): Target Scanner →  stand at side and call "LOW /
MED / HIGH" for your partnerʼs next throw twice correctly
Rejoin immediately
Block 6 (14 min) — Application Game: Touchdown 4v4
Goal: communication + teamwork under game speed
Setup
Teams of 4 with flags on
Use a small field with an end zone
How to Play (clean, kid-friendly)
Offense huddles quickly and chooses a plan (who might have the ball / whoʼs
the decoy).
On "GO," offense tries to get into the end zone.
Defense pulls flags to stop progress.
Once someone reaches the end zone (or play ends), offense reveals who had
the ball.
Scoring (gamefied for Week 2)
1 point = touchdown (ball successfully gets to end zone)

Differentiation
Easier
