Week 6 — Offensive Strategy:
Play Action + 4th Down

Theme:Strategy Makes Us Hard to Guard
DR Focus: one main cue, high reps, fast resets, no sitting JOB ZONE
Cue of the Week ONE cue only):"SELL THE FAKE."
What "SELL THE FAKE" means (kid version)
RB takes the fake like itʼs real: eyes upfield + run steps (donʼt stop)
QB acts like itʼs a real run first, then passes (we donʼt coach QB today—just
mention)
Coach words (every rep):
👉  "FAKE IS REAL… SELL IT!"
Observable proof
RB takes 23 real run steps after the fake (not a jog, not standing)
RB doesnʼt turn into a receiver too early

### Page 3

How to play (as written, simplified)
Offense tries to reach opposite goal line without getting flags pulled
If a flag is pulled: offensive player returns to their side, puts flag back on, tries
again
Retrieval cue (from Week 5"Elbow up pocket" (say once only during first
minute)
JOB ZONE if your flag is pulled →  quick job →  back in
Block 3 (7 min) — Skill Clarity (new cue)
Play Action Pass — 20–30s clarity demo
Curriculum defines Play Action as: QB fakes handoff to RB, RB goes out for a
pass.
Teach the sequence (super short):
QB and RB start like itʼs a run
RB sells the fake 23 run steps)
RB "leaks" out as receiver
QB throws
ONE cue only RB
👉  "SELL THE FAKE."
CFU 5 seconds):
"Freeze—show me 2 run steps after the fake." Everyone demos in place.)
Block 4 (12 min) — Fluency Reps (high reps, low talk)
Play Action "On Air" Reps (no defender)
Aligns with "practice executing play action skills… switch roles… multiple roles.")

### Page 4

Optional: skip the snap and start with "Set-Hut" to reduce fumbles (curriculum
suggests this option in small-group play).
Rep pattern (rotate every 2 plays)
Play 1 Fake →  RB leaks →  short throw to RB
Play 2 Fake →  RB leaks →  short throw to WR
Keep throws easy. Success > difficulty.)
Rep goal
8 play-action reps per group (fast)
Coach says only
👉  "SELL THE FAKE!" (reward the 23 real steps)
Common errors + fixes
RB turns too early →  "Two run steps first."
Fake looks slow →  "Fake is REAL speed."
QB rushes throw →  "Hold one heartbeat" (say once, then back to RB cue)

Still coached by the same cue; now with pressure.)

Add one defender whose ONLY job is to watch RB at walk pace
Defender canʼt rush QB; just "reads" RB
Rule
If RB doesnʼt sell it (no real steps), defender calls "READ!" and that rep doesnʼt
count.

Score (per group)

Block 6 (14 min) — Application / Constraint Game
4th Down (Offense Only) (curriculum "key activity")
Goal: group of 4 works together to score on each "4th down."
Setup
Fields/grids: 2025 yards each (as written)
Groups of 4 rotate QB/WR/RB/Center
How to run (simple)
Each QB chooses & calls one play; run 4 plays total, then rotate QB (matches
curriculum).
Require teams to include:
1 run
1 pass
1 play action
1 "best play" (their choice)
Scoring that rewards the cue
2 points = TD
1 bonus = Play Action TD where RB sold the fake
1 bonus = team rotates positions cleanly (no arguing; quick reset)
