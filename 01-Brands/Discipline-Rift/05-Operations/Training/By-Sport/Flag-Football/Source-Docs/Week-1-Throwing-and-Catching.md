### Page 8

Block 5 (13 min) — Decision Reps: "Call the Catch"
Same partners, same spots — now add a tiny decision.
How to Play
Thrower calls LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH before throwing
Receiver must show the correct hands:
LOW = rake
MED/HIGH = diamond
Switch roles every 3 throws
Coach focus stays the same:STEP to your target (donʼt add more throw cues)
Success criteria:
Thrower steps to target
Receiver shows correct hands + squeezes
Block 6 (14 min) — Application Game: Mascot Ball
(Groups of 4)
Setup
Groups of 4
QB in the middle, 3 receivers around (triangle)
How to Play
QB throws to a receiver
Receiver catches using correct hands, squeezes, tucks it away, and yells the
mascot/team word
Receiver throws back to QB
Continue until all 3 receivers catch

### Page 9

Rotate QB each round so everyone plays QB and receiver
Scoring (gamefied + cue-based)
Touchdown = 3/3 catches in a round
Bonus point if QB shows STEP to target on every throw that round
"Out" Rule (DR Job Zone — keep the game moving)
If a pass is dropped or off-target:
Player goes to JOB ZONE for 10 seconds
Quick Job (primary): Instant Coach
Tell the next thrower: "STEP to your target" + quick demo step
Re-entry: done →  back in on next turn
Backup Quick Job: Catch Hands Demo
Show diamond and rake hands once each
Re-entry: done →  back in
Coach script:
"JOB! Coach the cue. Back in fast."
