## Why this note exists
This note describes the most important end-to-end operating flows across the three Discipline Rift systems.

It is for understanding business motion, not code.

## Flow 1 — Parent visibility and trust
1. Parent authenticates
2. Parent sees children, programs, practices, and messages
3. Parent sees progress and payment history
4. Parent gains confidence and stays informed

Primary surface: [[Parent-App-Home]]

## Flow 2 — Coach session execution
1. Coach logs in
2. Coach selects team
3. Coach selects session occurrence
4. Coach records attendance
5. Coach communicates with parents if needed
6. Unread states and notifications update

Primary surface: [[Coach-Portal-Home]]

## Flow 3 — Program operations
1. Admin creates or edits schools, staff, teams, sessions, pricing, and capacity
2. Admin manages enrollments
3. Admin exports roster and oversees schedule health
4. Admin uses reminders and campaigns when operations need follow-up

Primary surface: [[Admin-Operations-App-Home]]

## Flow 4 — Parent communication at scale
1. Admin selects teams and recipients
2. Admin sends email or SMS campaigns with variables
3. Parents receive team-specific communication

Primary surface: [[Admin-Operations-App-Home]]

## Flow 5 — Attendance follow-up
1. Attendance is expected from coaches
2. Missing attendance triggers coach reminders
3. Student absences can trigger parent notifications
4. Weekly reports summarize attendance outcomes

Primary surfaces:
- [[Coach-Portal-Home]]
- [[Admin-Operations-App-Home]]

## Flow 6 — Certification and completion
1. Admin selects sport and team
2. Eligible students are selected
3. Certificates are generated and bundled
4. Completion becomes visible as an organizational output

Primary surface: [[Admin-Operations-App-Home]]

## Flow 7 — CRM sync
1. Parent and enrollment data exist internally
2. Admin or process triggers sync
3. GHL receives created/updated contacts and tags
4. CRM stays aligned with live enrollment state

Primary surface: [[Admin-Operations-App-Home]]

## Strategic reading of the system
- Parent App = trust and transparency
- Coach Portal = field execution
- Admin Operations App = business control
- Shared entities = structure
- Operational flows = motion

This note should be used whenever someone needs to understand not just what each app is, but how the business actually moves across them.
