## Voice
- Warm
- Elegant
- Inviting
- Sensory
- Refined but not stiff
- Confident without sounding luxury-for-luxury’s-sake

## Tone rules
- For menu copy: appetizing, visual, concise
- For catering copy: reassuring, polished, practical
- For story copy: intimate, rooted, family-centered
- For contact / conversion copy: simple, action-clear

## Tone should avoid
- Overly corporate phrasing
- Overly generic foodie language
- Empty hype
- Long poetic paragraphs with no action
- Confusing premium language that slows conversion

## Good tone examples
- “European foods for moments that matter.”
- “A collection of European-inspired favorites made for sharing.”
- “Visual social experience.”
- “We love to share.”

## Tone goal
The site should feel cinematic in style, but operational in clarity.
