## Confirmed constraints
- Order flow and pickup must route through the current live ordering path
- Uber Eats should be shown first among delivery options
- Catering requires clear distinction from standard menu
- 30 guest minimum for grazing-table style catering
- Standard booking notice: 1 week
- Custom setups may require up to 15 days
- 50% deposit to confirm
- Remaining balance due 24h before event
- Final quotes by Luis Torres
- Custom Styled Package should not be sold as instant checkout for now

## Operational implication
Do not design copy or flows that suggest universal instant booking for every catering option.
