## Primary avatars

### 1. Hungry local buyer
- In or near Avalon Park
- Wants something attractive and satisfying
- Decides quickly
- Cares about convenience, menu clarity, and where to order

### 2. Event planner / host
- Looking for catering for birthdays, showers, celebrations, gatherings, work events
- Cares about aesthetics, reliability, minimums, booking conditions, and cost clarity
- Needs confidence that the setup will look good and arrive correctly

### 3. Brand-aware food buyer
- Notices visuals, presentation, and storytelling
- May become repeat customer because the brand feels distinct
- More likely to share or post the food socially

### 4. Returning catering client
- Already understands the product
- Wants easier rebooking, clear package comparison, and fewer questions

## Buyer motivations
- “I want something that looks special.”
- “I need food that feels shareable and memorable.”
- “I want this to be easy.”
- “I want to know exactly what to do next.”
- “I want to feel good about ordering from this brand.”

## Buyer anxieties
- Is this regular menu or catering?
- How much does catering actually cost?
- What’s the minimum?
- How far in advance do I need to book?
- Is pickup easy?
- Is this worth the premium?
