## Common menu objections
- “Where do I actually order?”
- “Do they do pickup or only apps?”
- “What location is this for?”

## Common catering objections
- “What’s the minimum?”
- “How much does this cost per guest?”
- “How far ahead do I need to book?”
- “What’s included?”
- “Do I need to pay in full?”
- “What’s the difference between packages?”

## Objection handling direction
The website should resolve these before a human conversation where possible:
- routing clarity
- package explanation
- pricing anchor
- deposit expectations
- booking conditions
- contact path
