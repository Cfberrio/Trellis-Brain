## Core rules
- Prefer short, elegant lines over long explanations
- Use sharing / gathering language naturally
- Use “European-inspired” consistently
- Use “Book Now” only for catering
- Use “Order Now” only for menu / location order intent
- Keep form language simple and direct

## Menu writing rules
- Name first
- Appetite / texture / vibe second
- Do not overload with ingredient-heavy paragraphs unless needed
- Keep scannable on mobile

## Catering writing rules
- Lead with the experience and format
- Then clarify size, minimum, pricing logic, and booking conditions
- Do not hide operational constraints

## Contact / form writing rules
- Always use `info@cheesetoshare.us`
- Make the next step explicit
- Do not bury the call to action

## Avoid
- “unleash flavor”
- “journey through Europe”
- fake luxury copy
- unclear package names without explanation
