## Positioning Statement
Cheese To Share is a European-inspired food brand in Orlando that turns menu items and catering into **shareable, presentation-driven experiences** for everyday cravings and meaningful events.

## What CTS is
- A restaurant / food brand with a strong visual and experiential layer
- A catering brand with packaged and semi-custom grazing offers
- A brand that sits between convenience and occasion
- A brand that wins through aesthetics, curation, and warmth

## What CTS is not
- Not generic grab-and-go food
- Not a mass-market “cheap trays” catering brand
- Not a hyper-formal luxury catering company
- Not a broad international food concept with no point of view

## Positioning pillars
1. **European-inspired**
2. **Made for sharing**
3. **Beautifully presented**
4. **Useful for real moments**
5. **Warm and rooted**

## Competitive angle
The differentiation is not just menu items. It is the combination of:
- shareable formats
- strong plating/presentation
- emotional warmth
- catering readiness
- story and roots
- polished digital path from discovery to order / inquiry

## Website implication
Every page should reinforce:
- visual appetite
- ease of next step
- premium-but-friendly tone
- clear separation between daily menu and catering
