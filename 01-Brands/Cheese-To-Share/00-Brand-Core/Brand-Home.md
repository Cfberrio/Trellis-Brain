## Brand Summary
Cheese To Share is a Mediterranean / European-inspired food brand built around shareable experiences, catering, and visual presentation. The operating center of gravity is not “fine dining” or “fast food”; it is **beautiful, shareable, occasion-friendly food** that works both for everyday ordering and for moments that feel slightly elevated.

## What the brand sells
1. **Regular menu** for individual / small-group purchase.
2. **Catering menu** for events, especially visual food experiences.
3. **Pickup / delivery convenience** through third-party apps and direct pickup flow.
4. **A story-rich brand experience** anchored in family roots, sharing, and presentation.

## Business model snapshot
- Everyday demand is captured through **Menu + Location + Order Now**.
- Higher-ticket demand is captured through **Catering + Book Now + inquiry flow**.
- The website’s job is dual:
  - convert immediate food intent into orders
  - convert event intent into qualified catering leads

## Core brand promise
European foods for moments that matter. We love to share.

## Brand priorities
- Keep the website elegant but not confusing.
- Keep the menu attractive but scannable.
- Keep catering premium-feeling but operationally simple.
- Route daily order intent fast.
- Route catering intent into a structured lead capture + follow-up system.

## Strategic truth
CTS should feel more elevated than a typical local restaurant site, but still practical enough that a hungry user or event planner can act in under a minute.
