## Key facts captured
Global site structure: main navigation Menu, Catering, Location, Story, Contact, with Order Now as primary CTA and Book Now as catering CTA. The intended scroll flow is Hero > Menu > Catering > Location > Story > Contact/Footer. The Hero must use “Cheese To Share,” “European foods for moments that matter,” and “We love to share.” The Menu page should route Order Now to Location; Location centers on Avalon Park with Uber Eats first, then DoorDash and Pickup; Catering is separate from Menu and uses GoHighLevel for form capture plus admin SMS/app notifications. Booking conditions: 30 guest minimum, 1 week notice standard, up to 15 days for custom, 50% deposit, balance 24h before event, and final quotes by Luis Torres. Contact email everywhere: info@cheesetoshare.us.
