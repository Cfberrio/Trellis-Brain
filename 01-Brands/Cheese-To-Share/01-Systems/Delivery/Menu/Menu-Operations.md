# Menu Operations — Carta Cheese to Share

## Purpose
Operational menu workbook. Contains current and historical CTS menus across multiple versions, languages, and outlets (DOWNTOWN). Drives front-of-house printed menus and POS item mapping.

## Source integrity notes
- Workbook has **8 sheets** spanning multiple versions and languages.
- Version trail visible in sheet names: `Inicial ESP V 06202025` (June 20 2025) → `ENG V 09242025` (Sept 24 2025) → `CartaFinal ESP23092025` (Sept 23 2025).
- `DOWNTOWN` sheet = location-specific variant.
- `V1 XXX`, `V2`, `V2 Tabla de quesos` = working drafts.
- `Carta ESP fotos` = photo-annotated menu.
- Sheets are not perfectly aligned column-wise — preserved verbatim per sheet.
- Menu PDF reference: `Menu.pdf` (top level of CTS-IMPORT) is the printed/finalized export. Not duplicated here — reference only.
