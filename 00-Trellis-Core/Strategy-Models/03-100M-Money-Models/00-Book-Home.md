## Purpose
Hub for the Hormozi book *$100M Money Models*. Holds distilled framework notes derived from this book. The book answers a specific question — *how do you get customers to pay enough, fast enough, that cash stops being a constraint on growth* — through a deliberate **sequence of offers** (a Money Model), not a single offer. Frameworks here are operationally distinct from `$100M Offers`: that book builds one Grand Slam Offer; this book sequences many offers across the customer's first 30 days.

This hub does not contain raw book content. Provenance is carried via `source_reference` frontmatter on each canonical note plus the Source Map.

## Canonical Home Rule
The four-offer Money Model architecture (Attraction / Upsell / Downsell / Continuity) is **canonical here**, not in `$100M Offers`. `$100M Offers` defines a single Grand Slam Offer; this book sequences offers across the customer journey. The two are complementary, not duplicative.

Concepts that are **canonical in `01-100M-Offers/`** and only referenced or applied here (do not duplicate):
- [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Bonuses|Bonuses]] — Continuity Bonus Offers apply this mechanism to the continuity slot.
- [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Guarantees|Guarantees]] — Feature Downsells use guarantee removal as a price-feature lever; Pay Less Now or Pay More Later uses a conditional satisfaction guarantee.
- [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Pricing-Power|Pricing Power]] — Anchor Upsell operationalizes the same anchoring logic at the upsell slot.
- [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Value-Equation|Value Equation]] — every Money Model offer ultimately moves one of its four variables; treated as foundation, not redefined.
