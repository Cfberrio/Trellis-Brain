## Reuse Rule (vs. `01-100M-Offers`)
- **Pricing power.** Branding restates that brand enables 2x–10x premium pricing. The canonical pricing model lives in `01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Pricing-Power`. [[Hormozi-Brand-Economics|Brand Economics]] links to it and adds the *mechanism by which a brand earns the right to that pricing* (pairing → transfer → association). Material extension justifies the local note.
- **Avatar / market selection.** The 4 criteria here (Growing, In Pain, Can Afford, Easy to Target — "GMEP") are operational targeting criteria that overlap with `Hormozi-Starving-Crowd`. Treated as *application/specialization*: documented inside [[Hormozi-Brand-Building-Steps|Brand Building Steps]] with a link to `Hormozi-Starving-Crowd`; not duplicated as a separate canonical note.
- **Value Equation, Grand Slam Offer, Scarcity, Urgency, Bonuses, Guarantees, Naming.** Not extended in this book — no notes created.
