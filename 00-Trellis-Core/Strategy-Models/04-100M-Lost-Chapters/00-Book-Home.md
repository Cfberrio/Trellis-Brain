## Reuse Rule
Concepts that already have a canonical home in `01-100M-Offers` are **not duplicated here**. Lost Chapters often references them; the Source Map maps those references to the existing canonical notes:

- Grand Slam Offer / Value Equation / Bonuses / Guarantees / Scarcity / Urgency / Naming → canonical in `01-100M-Offers`. Lost Chapters reuses them as building blocks.
- "Pricing posture" and "starving crowd" → canonical in `01-100M-Offers`. Lost Chapters does not extend them.

Canonical notes in this folder cover material that is genuinely new (Lost Chapters originated it) or materially distinct from the `01-100M-Offers` treatment (e.g., Offer-Stacking is sales-choreography over multiple Grand Slam Offers, distinct from Offer-Creation's single-offer construction method).
