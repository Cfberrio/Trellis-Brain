## Purpose
Hub for the Hormozi playbook *$100M Playbook: Pricing — Instant Ways to Make More Profit in Any Business*. Holds distilled framework notes derived from this playbook. Operating thesis of the book: **profit is unnatural — you must force it into existence**, and pricing is the single highest-leverage variable for doing it. Improving price 1% is ~2× as efficient at increasing profit as improving retention and ~4× as efficient as improving acquisition (Profitwell, 512-company study).

This book is mostly tactical: a lens (why pricing dominates the profit equation), a pricing model (value-based pricing + a measurement method), a set of operating rules, and ten low-risk pricing tactics ("Instant Profit Pricing Plays") that lift revenue 26.8%–63.8% combined without operational change.

This hub does not contain raw book content. Provenance is carried via `source_reference` frontmatter on each canonical note plus the Source Map.
