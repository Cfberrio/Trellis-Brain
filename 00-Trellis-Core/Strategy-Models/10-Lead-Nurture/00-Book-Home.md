## Purpose
Hub for the Hormozi playbook *$100M Playbook: Lead Nurture — How to get more leads to respond, schedule, and show*. Holds distilled framework notes derived from this playbook. Lead Nurture is a tactical playbook focused on **medium-term lead nurture** — the window between advertising and sales — with a single optimization target: **maximize 30-day show rates**. Built on four empirically-validated drivers of show-up rate (Availability, Speed, Personalization, Volume) plus an execution culture chapter.

This hub does not contain raw book content. Provenance is carried via `source_reference` frontmatter on each canonical note plus the Source Map.

## Reuse vs. New Decisions (vs. completed Hormozi books)
Lead Nurture covers a distinct operational layer (post-ad, pre-sale show-rate optimization) not addressed by the completed reference books:

- `01-100M-Offers/` covers **what to sell**. Lead Nurture covers **getting opted-in leads to actually show**. No overlap.
- `03-100M-Money-Models/` and `04-100M-Lost-Chapters/` cover acquisition economics and offer architecture. Lead Nurture sits one layer downstream — it assumes the lead already opted in. No overlap.
- `05-Branding/` covers reputation and recognition. No overlap with show-rate mechanics.
- `06-Fast-Cash/` runs on warm lists with promo sequences. The Lead Nurture pillars (especially Speed and Volume) are *prerequisites* for any Fast Cash play to convert; Fast Cash references warm-audience contact, not nurture mechanics. No content overlap.
- `04-100M-Lost-Chapters/Hormozi-Lead-Getting-Employees` is about *hiring* lead-getters; this book is about *processing* leads after they opt in. No overlap.

All seven notes in this folder are genuinely new canonical material. No duplication of existing notes; no link-out to canonical homes elsewhere is required for the substantive content.
