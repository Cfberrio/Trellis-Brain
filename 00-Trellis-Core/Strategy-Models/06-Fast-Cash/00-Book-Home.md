## Purpose
Hub for the Hormozi playbook *$100M Playbook: Fast Cash — The Fastest Way to Rip Cash Out of a Business*. Holds distilled framework notes derived from this playbook. Fast Cash is a short, tactical playbook centered on one operating thesis: **revenue is vanity, profit is sanity, cash is reality** — and on a single repeatable mechanism (a limited, premium-priced, time-boxed offer sent to warm audiences) that converts existing customer demand into immediate cash.

This hub does not contain raw book content. Provenance is carried via `source_reference` frontmatter on each canonical note plus the Source Map.

## Reuse vs. New Decisions (vs. 01-100M-Offers)
This playbook reuses Hormozi's enhancement levers — scarcity, urgency, bonuses — without redefining them. They remain canonical in `01-100M-Offers/`. This book contributes a distinct mechanism (premium offer to warm audience on a 7-day clock) and a distinct ratio (10x the 10%). Where it reuses 100M Offers concepts, the canonical link is preserved:

- **Scarcity / Urgency / Bonuses** — defined in `01-100M-Offers/`. Fast Cash uses them as inputs; canonical home stays there. See [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Scarcity|Scarcity]], [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Urgency|Urgency]], [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Bonuses|Bonuses]].
- **Offer Creation Process** — Fast Cash explicitly references "Offer Creation Process Part I & II in $100M Offers" as the construction tool for the offer body. See [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Offer-Creation|Offer Creation]].
- **Grand Slam Offer architecture** — Fast Cash offers are GSO-shaped for a warm-audience, time-boxed context. Canonical GSO definition stays in `01-100M-Offers`. See [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Grand-Slam-Offer|Grand Slam Offer]].
