# Marketing Machine — Book Home

## Parent
- [[../Hormozi-Home|Hormozi Home]]

## Purpose
Hub for the Hormozi playbook *$100M Playbook: Marketing Machine — How to Turn Your Business Into an Ads Machine*. Holds distilled framework notes derived from this playbook. The book documents one operating thesis: **the bottom of the customer-journey list feeds the top of the ad list** — i.e., support, onboarding, community, events, awards, and competitions are not just delivery mechanics, they are the production line for customer-generated ad creative. The Marketing Machine is a checklist of nodes that, once installed, delivers fresh proof-based ads to the operator's inbox every week without the founder needing to be the face.

This hub does not contain raw book content. Provenance is carried via `source_reference` frontmatter on each canonical note plus the Source Map.
