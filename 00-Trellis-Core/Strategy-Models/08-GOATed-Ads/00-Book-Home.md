# GOATed Ads — Book Home

## Purpose
Hub for the Hormozi playbook *$100M Playbook: GOATed Ads — How to Make Ads That Get the Right People to Click*. Holds distilled framework notes derived from this playbook. The book sits on top of the paid ads chapters in *$100M Leads* and operationalizes a single thesis: ad scale is bottlenecked by ad *quality*, not audience size, and quality is produced by a high-volume assembly line (50 hooks × 3–5 meats × 1–3 CTAs = 150–750 ads per weekly recording session) targeted across an awareness continuum.

This hub does not contain raw book content. Provenance is carried via `source_reference` frontmatter on each canonical note plus the Source Map.

## Reuse vs. New Decisions
The CTA chapter explicitly says enhancement levers (urgency, scarcity, guarantees, bonuses) "apply here, and everywhere else you tell your audience to do something." Canonical home for those levers stays in `01-100M-Offers/`. The book also explicitly distinguishes its own hook section from the *Best Hooks of All Time Playbook* (extracted in this vault as `07-Hooks/`). This book links to both rather than duplicating.

- **Scarcity / Urgency / Bonuses / Guarantees** — defined in `01-100M-Offers/`. GOATed Ads uses them as CTA enhancers; canonical home stays there. See [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Scarcity|Scarcity]], [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Urgency|Urgency]], [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Bonuses|Bonuses]], [[../01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Guarantees|Guarantees]].
- **Word-level hook construction / all-time hook library / 70-20-10 distribution / hook types palette** — defined in `07-Hooks/` (the *Best Hooks of All Time Playbook*). GOATed Ads explicitly says its hook section is *not* about word types or the all-time hook list — it is about **sourcing hooks for paid ads** + mapping them to **awareness levels**. Distinct scope; canonical home for the word/library/distribution material stays in `07-Hooks/`. See [[../07-Hooks/Hormozi-Hook-Library|Hook Library]], [[../07-Hooks/Hormozi-Hook-Types-Palette|Hook Types Palette]], [[../07-Hooks/Hormozi-Hook-Definition|Hook Definition]], [[../07-Hooks/Hormozi-Hook-70-20-10-Process|Hook 70/20/10 Process]].

The book also references *$100M Leads* (paid ads chapters), the *Marketing Machine Playbook* (lifecycle ads), and the forthcoming *Kaleidoscope Ads* playbook. None of these are extracted in this vault; they are cited where relevant but are not canonical here.

## Canonical Home Rule
The five frameworks above are canonical in this folder. They are specific to ad creation mechanics (assembly, awareness targeting, hook sourcing for paid ads, meat formats, CTA construction) and are not duplicated elsewhere in the vault. CTA enhancement levers (scarcity, urgency, bonuses, guarantees) remain canonical in `01-100M-Offers/`. Word-level hook construction, the all-time hook library, the hook types palette, and the 70/20/10 distribution process remain canonical in `07-Hooks/`. This book links out rather than re-canonicalizing.
